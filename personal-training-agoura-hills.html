A Private Studio, Not A Crowded Gym

Fitness Brainiac is a private personal training studio in Agoura Hills, tucked just off Agoura Road near the 101. Clients come here to train one-on-one — no waiting for equipment, no distractions, no membership crowd. Whether you're driving in from Oak Park, Calabasas, Westlake Village, Thousand Oaks, or down from Lake Sherwood, the studio is a short, easy trip.

Every program is built around the individual. Agoura Hills clients range widely: professionals rebuilding strength after years at a desk, parents training through and after pregnancy, older adults working on stability and fall prevention, and athletes chasing competitive calisthenics goals. The common thread is coaching that adapts to your body and your life, grounded in over two decades of hands-on experience.

What We Train

Strength, fat loss, postural correction, pain mitigation, power, pre- and post-natal, competitive calisthenics, and strategic body recomposition.

Who Trains Here

Agoura Hills, Oak Park, Calabasas, Westlake Village, Thousand Oaks, Newbury Park, Lake Sherwood, and Malibu residents.

How It Works

Private one-on-one and semi-private sessions in 60- and 30-minute formats. Packages scale from a single session to 24.
