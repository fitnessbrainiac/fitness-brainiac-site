// SEASONAL PROGRAM

Mind Forward

Mind Forward isn't a lecture series, and it isn't just meditation. It's a community for people who want to live with more intention — a seasonal program where sharpening self-awareness becomes the foundation for real self-efficacy, in the gym and everywhere else.

// WHAT IT IS

A Community, Not A Class

People gathering with shared intention — meditation practice and honest conversation as tools for self-awareness, not a passive lecture. The goal isn't to sit still for an hour; it's to leave with a sharper sense of who you are and how you want to act.

// WHY IT MATTERS

Self-Awareness Into Self-Efficacy

Knowing yourself is the starting point, not the goal. Mind Forward is built to turn that awareness into real confidence in your own ability to follow through — in training, at work, and in the rest of your life.

// HOW IT RUNS

Seasonal Cohorts

Offered a few times per year rather than continuously, so each cohort gets real attention and the group actually becomes a community. Enrollment windows and session details are announced ahead of each season — reach out to be notified.
